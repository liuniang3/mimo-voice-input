### Design - MiMo Voice Input Tray Icon

Type: app icon / tray icon
Platform or ratio: 1:1 square, Windows tray and app icon, optimized for 16px, 32px, 64px, 256px
Palette: deep ink #111827, clear cyan #38D5FF, voice blue #2563EB, clean white #F8FAFC
Text: no text

```text
A polished finished design asset for MiMo Voice Input, a compact Windows voice dictation input method.

CANVAS AND COMPOSITION:
Square icon composition on a transparent background, centered geometric mark with strong silhouette readability at very small sizes. Use a rounded microphone capsule as the central anchor, with a subtle text-caret shape integrated into the microphone stem and two short sound-wave arcs framing the upper sides. Keep generous outer padding so the icon is legible in the Windows taskbar tray.

MAIN SUBJECT:
A simplified microphone combined with text input and speech waves: the microphone head reads instantly as voice, the lower stem resolves into a crisp input cursor, and the side arcs suggest live speech recognition. The design should feel precise, lightweight, and modern rather than decorative.

LAYOUT AND TYPOGRAPHY:
No letters, no rendered words, no fake UI text. The icon must work as a standalone symbol. Use bold simple shapes with consistent stroke weight, clean internal spacing, and high contrast between foreground and background.

COLOR, LIGHT, AND MATERIAL:
Use a restrained blue-to-cyan accent over a dark ink base with small white highlights only where needed for contrast. Flat vector-like material with slight depth from layered fills, not glossy 3D. Preserve clarity when downscaled to 16px.

STYLE CONSTRAINTS:
No watermark, no brand hallucination, no complex gradients, no tiny details, no speech bubbles, no keyboard clutter, no realistic microphone mesh, no background scene. Icon should be export-ready for PNG and ICO use.
```
